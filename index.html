Colour A 2D Platforming Video Game

Colour, a two-dimensional platforming video created in Unity 2D. With a familiar retro feel, the game offers a fun story and gameplay experience for players of all ages.

The game is simple in nature, utilising simple, easy to pick up controls and a simple objective; collect 7 different coins throughout each level whilst overcoming obstacles and enemies.

Colour was heavily inspired by older platforming video games, such as Super Mario Bros, and this is shown both in the aesthetic and gameplay of the game. It utilises a monochrome colour scheme (bar some objects) to help give a feeling of nostalgia.

Colour offers 3 distinct levels for the player to go through, which each level increasing in difficulty and a changing level aesthetic.

MTG Hub An eCommerce website

MTG Hub is an eCommerce store created to offer Magic: The Gathering fans a place to purchase various Magic: The Gathering products. Users can create an account with MTG Hub, browse product listings, add items to their cart, purchase items and have items sent to their address.

Simplicity, consistency and the overall user experience were kept in mind whilst designing the user interface.

All product and user information is stored within a database. Admins can access the information within this database through the website itself; updating/deleting/adding new products and managing users.

MTG Hub utilises the n-tier architecture to provide an eCommerce experience to users.
